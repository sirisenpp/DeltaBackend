Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Gas Sniffer?

Gas Sniffer is a platform that helps you monitor and check air quality and detect hazardous gases in the environment.

How do I use the Air Quality Check tool?

Simply visit the Air Quality Check page and enter the required parameters to get a detailed report on the air quality in your area.

How accurate is the gas detection system?

Our gas detection system uses high-quality sensors and real-time data analysis to provide accurate results.

Can I get notifications about air quality updates?

Yes! You can subscribe to notifications to receive updates about air quality changes and alerts about hazardous gases.
